Richard Honiker: Student, Brain Surgeon, Former Governor Of Colorado

Richard Honiker is a Junior at Champlain College, class of 2021. He is studying Computer Science, with a minor in Game Programming. Rumours is the eleventh studio album by British-American rock band Fleetwood Mac, released on 4 February 1977 by Warner Bros. Records. Largely recorded in California in 1976, it was produced by the band with Ken Caillat and Richard Dashut. The band wanted to expand on the commercial success of their eponymous 1975 album, but struggled with relationship breakups before recording started. The Rumours studio sessions were marked by hedonism and strife among band members that shaped the album's lyrics.
